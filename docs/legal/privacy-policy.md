## Information We Collect

### Information Provided by Teachers

When teachers create an account, we collect:
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Classroom Information:** Classroom names, grade levels, classroom codes
- **Educational Content:** Vocabulary worksheets, test materials uploaded by teachers
- **Usage Data:** Login times, feature usage, actions performed in the application

### Information About Students

We collect minimal information about students:
- **Basic Information:** First name, last name, username (no email required)
- **Classroom Enrollment:** Classroom membership information
- **Academic Data:** Test attempts, scores, answers submitted by students
- **Usage Data:** Login times, test completion times

**Important:** We do NOT collect:
- Student email addresses
- Student phone numbers
- Student home addresses
- Student dates of birth
- Any personally identifiable information beyond name and username

### Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Log Data:** IP addresses (anonymized), access times, pages viewed
- **Performance Data:** Error logs, application performance metrics

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use:
- **Essential Cookies:** For authentication and session management (required)
- **Analytics:** For understanding application usage and improving performance
- **Error Tracking:** For identifying and fixing technical issues

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### Compliance with COPPA

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for children under 13:

**Teacher/School Consent:**
- Student accounts are created by teachers or schools acting as agents for parents
- Teachers provide consent on behalf of parents under the "school official" exception
- We rely on schools to obtain necessary parental consent as required by law

**Minimal Data Collection:**
- We collect only information necessary for educational purposes
- No email addresses or contact information required for students
- No marketing or advertising to students

**Parental Rights:**
- Parents can request access to their child's information
- Parents can request deletion of their child's information
- Parents can refuse further collection of their child's information

### How Parents Can Exercise Rights

Parents or guardians can:
1. Contact their child's teacher or school administrator
2. Contact us directly at [privacy@vocab-app.example.com]
3. Request access, review, or deletion of their child's information

We will respond to parental requests within 30 days.

## Data Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security updates and patches
- Automated backup systems

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (teachers vs students)
- Multi-factor authentication (optional for teachers)
- Classroom-based data isolation
- Regular access audits

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Automated threat detection
- Regular security assessments
- Incident response procedures

### Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting personal information:
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
- We will notify appropriate authorities as required by law
- We will provide information about the breach and steps to protect yourself

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods

**Active Accounts:**
- Teacher accounts: Retained while account is active
- Student accounts: Retained while enrolled in active classroom
- Academic data: Retained for the duration of classroom enrollment

**Inactive Accounts:**
- Accounts inactive for 2 years may be deleted
- 30-day notice provided before deletion
- Users can reactivate accounts during notice period

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Account data deleted within 30 days of deletion request
- Backup copies deleted within 90 days
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained

**Legal Retention:**
- We may retain information longer if required by law
- We may retain information for legal claims or disputes
